Our clients recognise the value of these technologies and understand that the right skills are necessary to maximise the return on their technology investment.

About Vantage Point Solutions

vantage point solutions consultants have ten years experience delivering enterprise portal-based and web content management solutions to meet challenging client requirements.

Our consultants have worked closely with IBM and Adobe / Day to establish best-practices and processes for the sucessful delivery of solutions based on the Adobe CQ, IBM WebSphere Portal and IBM Web Content Management platforms.

We believe in working closely with our clients to adapt and expand their development process to suit portal and web content management development.

Our Services

  • Technical Leadership
    We can provide leadership and/or mentoring in Adobe CQ, IBM WebSphere Portal Server, IBM Web Content Management, WebSphere Application Server and the Rational software development platform (RAD / RSA).
